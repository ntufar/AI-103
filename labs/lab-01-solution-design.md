# Lab 01: AI Solution Design

## Objective

Choose the correct Azure AI services for three business scenarios and justify your choices.

## Scenarios

1. A support center needs automatic sentiment scoring and issue summarization.
2. A legal team needs semantic search across contracts with answer generation.
3. A manufacturing team needs OCR from equipment photos and alert generation.

## Your Task

For each scenario, provide:
- Recommended services
- Architecture flow (3-6 steps)
- 2 trade-offs (cost, latency, quality, risk)
- Security and responsible AI considerations

## Grading Rubric (5 points)

- 1 point: Correct service selection
- 1 point: Clear architecture flow
- 1 point: Trade-off quality
- 1 point: Security/compliance considerations
- 1 point: Responsible AI coverage

## Self Review

- [ ] Could I defend this design in under 2 minutes?
- [ ] Did I mention at least one risk and mitigation?
