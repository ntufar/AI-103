# 30-Day AI-103 Study Plan

This plan assumes 60-120 minutes per day.

## Week 1: Foundations and Architecture

Day 1:
- Read `study-guides/exam-objectives-map.md`
- Build your baseline score by answering 20 self-made questions

Day 2:
- Study Azure AI service categories and when to choose each
- Write 10 scenario-to-service mappings

Day 3:
- Study pricing, quotas, and regional availability concepts
- Create a one-page cost and scale decision tree

Day 4:
- Study responsible AI principles and common failure patterns
- Complete `labs/lab-01-solution-design.md`

Day 5:
- Review week notes and create flashcards (20+)

Day 6:
- Build or modify app code in `apps/text-analysis-cli/`

Day 7:
- Self-test and score in `labs/scorecard.md`

## Week 2: Generative AI and Prompting

Day 8:
- Study prompt structure: role, instruction, context, constraints, examples

Day 9:
- Run `apps/prompt-eval-trainer/main.py` and complete all scenarios

Day 10:
- Practice prompt rewrites for precision and safety

Day 11:
- Design a simple RAG flow on paper (query -> retrieve -> compose)

Day 12:
- Study output evaluation criteria: correctness, relevance, groundedness

Day 13:
- Complete `labs/lab-02-prompt-engineering.md`

Day 14:
- Mini mock exam: 25 questions

## Week 3: NLP, Vision, and Search

Day 15:
- Sentiment, key phrases, entities, and summarization review

Day 16:
- Extend text analysis app with one extra metric

Day 17:
- Vision concepts: OCR vs image tagging vs object detection use cases

Day 18:
- Knowledge mining and indexing pipeline concepts

Day 19:
- Retrieval quality tuning: chunk size, overlap, semantic rank

Day 20:
- Complete `labs/lab-03-nlp-vision-search.md`

Day 21:
- Mini mock exam: 30 questions

## Week 4: Integration and Exam Readiness

Day 22:
- Review all quick references in `quick-reference/`

Day 23:
- Re-do all missed or low-score lab tasks

Day 24:
- Build 15 architecture scenario answers

Day 25:
- Practice explaining trade-offs aloud (cost, latency, quality, risk)

Day 26:
- Full mock exam simulation (timeboxed)

Day 27:
- Analyze weak spots and targeted review

Day 28:
- Final run of both apps and score improvements

Day 29:
- Light review and formula-style memory refresh

Day 30:
- Final confidence check and exam-day strategy

## Success Criteria

- 80%+ on two consecutive mock sessions
- Consistent lab scores above 4/5 in each domain
- Able to justify solution choices with trade-offs in under 2 minutes
