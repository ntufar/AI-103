## If You Panic During Exam

**Deep breath strategy:**

1. **Physical:**
   - Take 3 deep breaths (in for 4, hold for 4, out for 4)
   - Roll shoulders, flex fingers
   - Stretch arms gently

2. **Mental:**
   - "I've prepared for this"
   - "One question at a time"
   - "I can figure this out"
   - "Pace is fine, no rush"

3. **Tactical:**
   - Skip the hard question (come back later)
   - Find an easy one to build confidence
   - Shake it off and move forward
